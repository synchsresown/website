Athens, Georgia-based label and multidisciplinary collective Primordial Void celebrates its 50th release with a 48-track mega-compilation, released in two parts. The first, Primordial God, brings together mostly folk and rock-oriented collaborators from the label’s sphere, whereas the second, Primordial Energy, focuses on a wide range of like-minded electronic experimentalists.
